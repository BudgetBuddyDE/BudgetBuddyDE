Everything you need

A complete toolkit for managing your personal finances — simple, powerful, and open-source.

Dashboard

Get a comprehensive overview of your financial health with real-time statistics, upcoming payments, and recent transactions.

Transaction Management

Easily record and categorize your income and expenses. Keep your financial records up to date with minimal effort.

Recurring Payments

Never miss a bill again. Track your subscriptions and regular payments in one place so nothing slips through the cracks.

Budgeting & Analytics

Visualize your spending habits with interactive charts. Monitor your budget adherence and identify areas to save.

Categories & Methods

Customize your financial tracking by managing your own categories and payment methods to fit your lifestyle.

Dark Mode

Fully supported dark mode for a comfortable user experience in any environment, day or night.
